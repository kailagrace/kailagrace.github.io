ABOUT KAILA

Hi there, I’m Kaila. I am a User Interaction Designer, and I make my products functional, memorable, usable, & beautiful. I’m a left coaster, currently living life in the East with my fantastic fiance, Billy, and our mountain bikes, Lil’ Oatie and Chase. I am passionate about learning, I love adventure, & I can’t turn down a challenge. When I'm not coding or creating my own digital designs, I can often be found doing one of the three R's: reading, running, or riding my bicycle. I enjoy cheesy puns, cooking 'experiment-style', NPR, pine forests, coffee dates, and dreaming of one day being a dog-owner. I graduated from the University of Connecticut in 2015, with a BS in Human-Computer Interaction, a major I created. In the past, I’ve worked at Columbia University, the University of Connecticut, and Tyler Technologies. Check out my blog. I love meeting new people, so feel free to reach out to me and say hello.
